## Production Notes

**General Guidelines for Suno 5.1:**

Each prompt is designed to generate a 2-3 minute loop-friendly track. For the game, these should be set to loop seamlessly. The Discovery Moment (Track 14) is the exception — it's a one-shot 15-second sting.

**Mixing Notes:**
- All tracks should sit at -14 LUFS for comfortable background listening
- Tracks 4 and 11 (combat themes) can push to -10 LUFS for intensity
- Track 14 (discovery sting) should hit -8 LUFS for maximum impact
- Leave headroom for Elara's VO to sit on top of any track

**Looping Strategy:**
- Ensure intro and outro sections can be trimmed for seamless looping
- The "break" sections in each track serve as natural transition points
- Combat tracks should have clear "victory" and "defeat" alternate endings

**Thematic Consistency:**
- All tracks share a minor key foundation (C minor / E minor family)
- The recurring 3-note motif from Track 16 (Awakening) should appear as an Easter egg in at least 3 other tracks
- Analog warmth + digital cold is the sonic palette of the entire game
