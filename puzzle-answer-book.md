## 1. Ark Exploration Puzzles

These puzzles are encountered in specific rooms aboard the Ark. Each must be solved to unlock the room's full contents and progress the story.

### 1.1 Bridge Power Relay

| Field | Details |
|-------|---------|
| **Room** | Bridge |
| **Type** | Power Relay (Binary Toggle) |
| **Puzzle** | Activate the correct relay sequence to restore main power |
| **Elara Hint** | "The emergency frequency follows a binary pattern based on the Ark's designation number — 47. In binary, that's 101111. Toggle the relays to match." |
| **Solution** | Toggle the 6 switches to match the binary representation of 47: **ON, OFF, ON, ON, ON, ON** (101111) |

### 1.2 Archives Access Protocol

| Field | Details |
|-------|---------|
| **Room** | Archives |
| **Type** | Riddle |
| **Puzzle** | Answer the riddle to gain access to the ship's archives |

**The Riddle:**

> I am the thread that connects all things,
> Yet I am invisible to the eye.
> I bind the powerful to the weak,
> The living to those who die.
> I am found in every story told,
> In every war and every peace.
> Without me, all would be forgotten —
> With me, nothing will cease.

**Answer:** **"lore"**

**Also accepted:** memory, history, knowledge, story, stories, narrative

**Elara Hint:** "Think about what connects all the data in this ship — every entity, every relationship, every secret. It's the thing that binds stories together."

### 1.3 Communications Decryption

| Field | Details |
|-------|---------|
| **Room** | Comms Array |
| **Type** | Caesar Cipher |
| **Cipher Text** | `WKH VDJD FRQWLQXHV` |
| **Cipher Key** | Shift-3 (move each letter back 3 positions) |
| **Solution** | **"the saga continues"** |
| **Elara Hint** | "It's a simple Caesar cipher — each letter is shifted by a fixed number. The signal header says 'SHIFT-3'. Move each letter back 3 positions in the alphabet." |

**Decryption walkthrough:**
- W → T, K → H, H → E → "THE"
- V → S, D → A, J → G, D → A → "SAGA"
- F → C, R → O, Q → N, W → T, L → I, Q → N, X → U, H → E, V → S → "CONTINUES"

## 3. Research Minigame — Puzzle Mechanics

The Research Minigame generates puzzles dynamically from the Loredex database. Here are the mechanics and strategies for each type.

### 3.1 Cipher Decode

| Mechanic | Details |
|----------|---------|
| **Goal** | Guess the hidden character name letter by letter |
| **Display** | Name shown as █ blocks (spaces and punctuation visible) |
| **Hint** | First sentence of the character's bio with name redacted |
| **Max Wrong Guesses** | 6 |
| **Reward** | 50 XP + character discovered in Loredex |

**Strategy:**
- Start with common letters: E, A, R, T, O, I, N, S
- The hint text often contains the character's affiliation or era — cross-reference with known lore
- Spaces in the encrypted name reveal word boundaries, helping narrow possibilities
- Characters with shorter names are easier; "The" prefix is very common

### 3.2 Connection Web

| Mechanic | Details |
|----------|---------|
| **Goal** | Match 4-5 characters to their known connections |
| **Display** | Characters on left, shuffled connections on right |
| **Scoring** | Each correct match counts; all correct = puzzle solved |
| **Reward** | 50 XP per puzzle completion |

**Strategy:**
- Use the Conspiracy Board as a reference — it shows all known connections
- Faction members tend to connect to faction leaders
- Songs connect to the characters they're about
- Locations connect to characters who reside there

### 3.3 Timeline Sequence

| Mechanic | Details |

| **Goal** | Arrange 4-5 characters in chronological era order |
| **Display** | Draggable cards with character names and eras |

## 4. Lore Quiz — Question Categories & Strategies

The Lore Quiz generates questions dynamically from the Loredex database. Questions fall into five categories.

### Question Types

| Type | Question Format | Difficulty |
|------|----------------|------------|
| Faction Affiliation | "Which faction is [character] affiliated with?" | Easy |
| Album Identification | "Which album features the song [title]?" | Easy |
| Era Identification | "In which era does [character] primarily appear?" | Medium |
| Entity Type | "What type of entity is [name]?" | Easy |
| Connection Exclusion | "Which of these is NOT directly connected to [entity]?" | Hard |

### Difficulty Levels

| Level | Questions | Time Limit | XP per Correct | Label |
|-------|-----------|------------|-----------------|-------|
| Easy (Recruit) | 10 | None | 5 XP | RECRUIT |
| Medium (Operative) | 15 | None | 10 XP | OPERATIVE |
| Hard (Commander) | 20 | 30 seconds each | 20 XP | COMMANDER |

### Strategy Tips

- **Faction questions:** Learn the major factions — The Panopticon (Empire), The Insurgency, The Brotherhood of the Ocularum, The Hierarchy of the Damned
- **Album questions:** There are 4 albums — Dischordian Logic, The Age of Privacy, The Book of Daniel 2:47, Silence in Heaven
- **Connection questions:** The "NOT connected" questions are hardest — use process of elimination
- **Streak bonuses:** Consecutive correct answers build a streak counter for bragging rights

## 8. War Map — Faction Strategy Guide

### Actions

| Action | Cost | Effect |
|--------|------|--------|
| **Capture** | 100 Influence Points | Claim an unclaimed or weakly held sector for your faction |
| **Reinforce** | 50 Influence Points | Strengthen your faction's hold on a sector you control |
| **Sabotage** | 75 Influence Points | Weaken the opposing faction's control of a sector |

### Earning Influence Points

- Complete Trade Empire missions
- Win card battles
- Discover new Loredex entries
- Complete Ark puzzles
- Participate in faction events

### Season Structure

- Seasons reset weekly
- At season end, the faction controlling more sectors wins
- Top contributors receive bonus rewards
- Territory control percentages are tracked on the leaderboard

### Faction Tips

**Empire (Red):** Focus on fortifying core sectors and building outward. Reinforce heavily — Empire strength is in defense.

**Insurgency (Blue):** Use sabotage aggressively to weaken Empire strongholds. Strike at the edges and expand quickly.
