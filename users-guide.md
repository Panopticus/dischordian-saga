## 1. Welcome to Loredex OS

Loredex OS is the official interactive companion app for **The Dischordian Saga**, a transmedia universe created by **Malkia Ukweli** spanning music, visual storytelling, and interactive fiction. The app serves as a classified intelligence database, an interactive game platform, and a gateway into the mythology of the Panopticon.

The Dischordian Saga is set in a universe where reality itself has fractured. Three species — the **DeMagi** (genetically enhanced humans), the **Quarchon** (vast artificial intelligences), and the **Ne-Yon** (perfect hybrids) — navigate a cosmos shaped by the conflict between the **Architect** (who seeks order through control) and the **Dreamer** (who champions chaos and free will).

You are a **Potential** — a being who has awakened aboard an **Inception Ark**, a vessel designed to preserve life through the Fall of Reality. Your guide is **Elara**, the Ark's AI, who will help you discover who you are, explore the ship, and uncover the secrets of the Dischordian universe.

## 2. Getting Started

### 2.1 First Launch — The Boot Sequence

When you first open Loredex OS, you will see a terminal-style boot sequence that initializes the system. This loads the database of entities, connections, and songs. Once complete, you arrive at the **Command Bridge** — the main dashboard.

### 2.2 Creating an Account

Loredex OS uses **Manus OAuth** for authentication. Click the login button to create an account or sign in. Your progress, discoveries, and game state are saved to your account.

### 2.3 The Awakening

New users are guided through the **Awakening** — an interactive character creation sequence where Elara, the Ark's AI, helps you:

1. Choose your **species** (DeMagi, Quarchon, or Ne-Yon)
2. Select your **class** (Engineer, Oracle, Assassin, Soldier, or Spy)
3. Declare your **alignment** (Order or Chaos)
4. Attune to an **element** (varies by species)
5. Choose your **name**

Your choices affect your starting deck in the card game, your character stats, and your visual identity throughout the app.

### 2.4 Wallet Connection (Optional)

During the Awakening, Elara will ask if you have an Ethereum wallet containing a **Potential** or **Ne-Yon** NFT. If you do, connecting your wallet unlocks:

- The **Ne-Yon** species option (requires Potentials NFT #1-10)
- Enhanced capabilities and exclusive content
- Your NFT identity bound to your citizen profile

This step is entirely optional. You can play the full game without an NFT.

## 5. The Lore — Database & Discovery

### 5.1 The Loredex Database

The Loredex contains **200+ entities** across multiple categories:

| Category | Description | Count |
|----------|-------------|-------|
| Characters | Key figures in the Dischordian universe | 50+ |
| Songs | Tracks across 4 albums | 89 |
| Locations | Planets, stations, realms | 20+ |
| Factions | Organizations and alliances | 15+ |
| Events | Historical moments | 20+ |
| Concepts | Lore concepts and technologies | 10+ |

### 5.2 Entity Dossiers

Each entity has a detailed dossier page containing:
- **Name and classification** (type, era, affiliation)
- **Portrait/image** (when available)
- **Biography** — Full lore description
- **Connections** — Links to related entities
- **Music** — Associated songs (for characters)
- **Music videos** — Official and Vevo links (for songs)
- **Era placement** — Where they fit in the timeline

### 5.3 Discovery System

Loredex OS features a **progressive discovery system**. Not all entries are visible from the start. You discover new entries by:

- Exploring the Ark and finding data crystals
- Completing puzzles in the Research Minigame
- Playing through the Card Game story mode
- Exploring sectors in Trade Empire
- Viewing entity pages (auto-discovers on visit)
- Completing Lore Quiz questions

Your **discovery percentage** is tracked on your Operative Dossier and contributes to your overall progress.

### 5.4 Conspiracy Board

The Conspiracy Board is a visual network graph showing connections between entities. Features include:

- **Discovery mode** (default) — Shows only discovered entities; undiscovered connections appear as mystery nodes with "???" labels and purple dashed borders
- **All mode** — Shows the complete network (toggle in toolbar)
- **Type filtering** — Filter by characters, songs, locations, factions, events
- **Zoom and pan** — Navigate the network with mouse/touch
- **Click to explore** — Click any node to view its dossier
- **Discovery progress** — Percentage indicator in the toolbar

### 5.5 Clue Journal

The Clue Journal tracks all clues you've collected during Ark exploration and shows your progress toward solving multi-room puzzles. Clues are categorized as:

- **Data Crystals** — Information stored on physical media found in rooms
- **Environmental** — Clues observed in the environment (displays, panels, markings)
- **Cross-Room** — Fragments that must be collected from multiple locations
- **Elara Hints** — Tips provided by Elara about specific puzzles
- **Achievement-Linked** — Clues that appear only after completing prerequisites

### 5.6 The Codex

The Codex is a deep lore library organized by topic. It provides extended reading on:
- The history of the Dischordian universe
- Species biology and culture
- Faction politics and conflicts
- Technology and artifacts
- The Fall of Reality and its aftermath

### 5.7 Timelines

Two timeline views are available:

- **Era Timeline** — Shows the progression of historical eras from Epoch Zero through the Age of the Potentials, with key events and characters placed chronologically
- **Character Timeline** — Focuses on individual character arcs, showing when they appear, their key moments, and how their stories intersect

## 7. Sagaverse Games

### 7.1 Simulation Hub

The Simulation Hub is the central launcher for all CADES (Collector's Arena Digital Entertainment System) simulations. It provides an overview of all available games with descriptions and quick-launch buttons.

### 7.2 Card Game

The card game is a faction warfare system where you build decks and battle opponents.

**Card Types:**
- Common, Rare, Epic, Legendary, Mythic rarities
- Attack, Defense, and Special ability stats
- Faction affiliations affect synergies
- Element bonuses based on your character's attunement

**Game Modes:**
- **Story Mode** — 12-chapter narrative following the Oracle's journey (see Chapter Guide in Answer Book)
- **Quick Battle** — Random opponent matches
- **PVP Arena** — Multiplayer battles against other players
- **Draft Tournament** — Build a deck from random picks and compete

**Deck Building:**
- Access via the Deck Builder page
- Minimum 20 cards per deck
- Maximum 3 copies of any card
- Balance attack, defense, and special abilities

### 7.3 Trade Empire

Trade Empire is an interstellar trade and exploration game set in a galaxy of **200 sectors**.

**Core Mechanics:**
- **Navigation** — Warp between connected sectors using the visual Galaxy Map or text commands
- **Trading** — Buy low, sell high across different sector types (ports, planets, stations)
- **Mining** — Extract resources from asteroid fields and nebulae
- **Colonization** — Establish colonies on habitable planets for passive income
- **Combat** — Engage pirates and hostile forces
- **Research** — Unlock technologies in the tech tree
- **Exploration** — Discover new sectors, relics, and sector events

**Galaxy Map:**
- SVG-based visual star map with pan and zoom
- Color-coded sectors by type (green=port, blue=planet, yellow=station, red=hazard, purple=nebula, cyan=wormhole)
- Warp lane connections shown as lines
- Current location highlighted with pulse animation
- Fog of war for undiscovered sectors
- Territory overlay showing faction claims (toggle with TERRITORIES button)
- Click adjacent sectors to warp directly

**Commands (Text Mode):**
- `map` — Open visual Galaxy Map
- `map-text` — Classic text-based sector list
- `warp [id]` — Travel to a connected sector
- `scan` — Scan current sector
- `trade` — Open trading interface
- `mine` — Mine resources (if available)
- `colonize` — Establish a colony
- `attack` — Engage hostiles
- `tech` — View tech tree
- `leaderboard` — View rankings
- `help` — Full command list

**Sector Events:**
Random events trigger when warping to new sectors, including ghost ships, pirate ambushes, distress signals, lore drops, anomalies, and wandering merchants. See the Answer Book for full event details.

### 7.4 War Map

The War Map is a faction territory control game where **Empire** and **Insurgency** players compete to claim sectors across the galaxy.

**Actions:**
- **Capture** (100 IP) — Claim an unclaimed or weakly held sector
- **Reinforce** (50 IP) — Strengthen your faction's hold
- **Sabotage** (75 IP) — Weaken the opposing faction

**Seasons reset weekly** with rewards for the winning faction and top contributors.

### 7.5 Explore the Ark

A point-and-click adventure aboard the Inception Ark. Explore rooms, find items, solve puzzles, and uncover the ship's secrets.

**Rooms:**
- Cryo Bay (starting room)
- Bridge
- Engineering
- Medical Bay
- Comms Array
- Navigation
- Armory
- Cargo Bay / Cargo Vault
- Mess Hall
- Training Arena
- War Room
- Archives / Restricted Archive
- Observation Deck
- Reactor Core
- Void Gate (endgame)
- Captain's Quarters

**Key Features:**
- Room descriptions with interactive elements
- Door markers showing connected rooms with directional labels (deck level indicators)
- Puzzle system integrated with the Clue Journal
- Items to collect and use across rooms
- Elara provides contextual guidance

### 7.6 Lore Quiz

Test your knowledge of the Dischordian universe across three difficulty levels:

| Level | Questions | Time Limit | XP per Correct |
|-------|-----------|------------|-----------------|
| Recruit (Easy) | 10 | None | 5 XP |
| Operative (Medium) | 15 | None | 10 XP |
| Commander (Hard) | 20 | 30 sec each | 20 XP |

Questions are generated dynamically from the Loredex database covering factions, albums, eras, entity types, and connections.

### 7.7 Research Puzzles (Minigame)

Three puzzle types that unlock new Loredex entries:

- **Cipher Decode** — Guess hidden character names letter by letter using bio hints
- **Connection Web** — Match characters to their known connections
- **Timeline Sequence** — Arrange characters in correct chronological era order

Each completed puzzle awards **50 XP** and discovers the featured entity in your Loredex.

### 7.8 Battle Arena & Combat Sim

- **Battle Arena** — Card combat against AI opponents with increasing difficulty
- **Combat Sim** — Training mode to practice combat mechanics
- **PVP Arena** — Real-time multiplayer card battles

### 7.9 Research Lab

Craft new cards by combining resources gathered from gameplay. Use materials from Trade Empire mining, Ark exploration, and battle rewards to create cards of increasing rarity.

## 13. Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Do I need an NFT to play?**
A: No. The full game is accessible without any NFT. NFTs unlock the Ne-Yon species and exclusive cosmetic content, but all gameplay features are available to everyone.

**Q: How do I unlock the Void Gate?**
A: The Void Gate is the endgame puzzle. You must solve every other puzzle on the Ark first. When all puzzles are complete, the Void Key materializes automatically.

**Q: What's the best species to choose?**
A: Each species has unique strengths. DeMagi excel at elemental combat, Quarchon at dimensional manipulation, and Ne-Yon have access to all elements but require an NFT. Choose based on your preferred playstyle.

**Q: How do I earn Dream Tokens faster?**
A: Focus on completing Ark puzzles (highest token rewards), winning card battles, and discovering new entities. The Void Gate puzzle alone awards 1,000 tokens.

**Q: Can I change my species/class after the Awakening?**
A: Character choices are permanent for your current citizen. You can create a new citizen to try different combinations.

**Q: How does the War Map reset work?**
A: Seasons reset weekly. All territory control is cleared, and the faction with the most sectors at reset time wins season rewards. Individual contribution scores carry over to the leaderboard.

**Q: What are the 4 albums?**
A: Dischordian Logic (2025), The Age of Privacy (2025), The Book of Daniel 2:47 (2025), and Silence in Heaven (2026). Together they contain 89 tracks that tell the story of the Dischordian universe.

**Q: How do I find hidden clues in the Ark?**
A: Explore every room thoroughly. Look for interactive elements, check the Clue Journal for hints about which rooms contain undiscovered clues, and ask Elara for guidance when stuck.

**Q: What happens when I reach 100% discovery?**
A: Reaching 100% discovery unlocks a special achievement, a unique trophy, and access to the Restricted Archive's deepest secrets. It also significantly boosts your leaderboard ranking.
